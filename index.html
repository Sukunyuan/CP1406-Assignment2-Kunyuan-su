Petite Treats

Welcome to Petite Treats bakery

We are Petite Treats, a small family-run local business, operating in Kirwan since 2012, We specialise in traditional Australian baked goods, slices, cakes, biscuits and desserts all made on site daily. You will also find a large selection of savoury lunch items such as quiches, meat pies, baguettes and wraps. Our bakery is located at 123 Prize Lane in Kirwan, Queensland.
